User Guide

For Customers:

  • Register as a Customer via the Register page.
  • Login using your Email and Password.
  • Book a ride using the "Book a Ride" option.
  • View your bookings under "My Bookings".

For Drivers:

  • Register as a Driver (optional vehicle assignment).
  • Login using your Driver Name and Password.
  • View and manage trips under "My Trips".
